VR Crane Simulator

Unity/C#

A VR simulation of a crane tutorial for industry project partner Packet39. For this project I acted as the lead to a four person team. We assisted Packet39 in creating an immersive training simulation for a crane operator. We designed the VR experience to mimic the control scheme and behavior of a real life crane while retaining all six degrees of freedom.

AR Spatial Anchor App

Unity/C#

As part of a three person team, I developed a mobile app that allows users to bind digital assets such as notes, videos and voice recordings relative to a spatial anchor (Azure). Primarily to be used by frontline workers to keep relevant information close at hand as well as give context (for industry project partner PTC).
